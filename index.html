Animal attractions

  • Tiger Trek

    • Come visit our resident sibling white tigers Keysha and Pasha at Tiger Trek which you can embark on just around the corner!

    Not easy being white
    White tigers are not albinos or a different sub-species of tigers. Their white coat and blue eyes are the result of a mutated gene, making them less efficient as predators since they hunt by stealth. Come take a closer look at these majestic tigers at Tiger Trek !





  • Wild africa

    • Africa boasts a diverse range of habitats, from tropical rainforests to harsh deserts. The animals found there have adapted accordingly in no end of amazing ways. As a result, the continent counts among its endemic species the tallest, the fastest and the largest creatures on land. These include:

    - Meerkats
    - Giraffes
    - White rhinoceros

    ... and many more!
